Our Story: Recognizing Nigeria's Sustainability Opportunity

In 2024, our founders recognized a critical gap in Nigeria's business landscape. While international ESG frameworks were rapidly evolving and Nigerian regulations were tightening, local businesses lacked access to expertise that truly understood both worlds.

Having worked with multinational corporations across Africa and witnessed the transformative power of well-implemented ESG strategies, we saw an unprecedented opportunity to help Nigerian businesses not just comply with regulations, but thrive in the new sustainable economy.

Today, we've helped over 50 Nigerian companies transform their sustainability practices, facilitated ₦2.5B+ in ESG investments, and maintained a 100% regulatory compliance rate with NGX and SEC requirements.
