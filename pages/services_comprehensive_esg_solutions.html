1. ESG & Sustainability Consulting

We help you integrate environmental, social, and governance factors into your core business strategy to identify and manage risks and capitalize on new opportunities.

  • ESG risk and opportunity analysis
  • Materiality and stakeholder engagement
  • ESG policy development
  • ESG governance structuring and board advisory
  • Sector-specific ESG benchmarking
  • Sustainable finance advisory (green/social bonds)

2. ESG & Sustainability Reporting & Disclosure

We create transparent and comprehensive sustainability reports that meet local and international standards, enhancing your reputation and stakeholder trust.

  • Sustainability report drafting (GRI, ISSB, NGX, UNGC, SASB)
  • Alignment with NGX Sustainability Disclosure Guidelines and FRC/IFRS S1/S2
  • Reporting for SMEs, listed companies, conglomerates
  • SDG mapping and impact narrative
  • Guidance on disclosure to avoid greenwashing

3. Industry-Specific ESG & Sustainability Framework Development

We design custom ESG frameworks tailored to your specific sector, ensuring compliance with relevant Nigerian and global regulations.

Tailored ESG frameworks for:

  • Manufacturing
  • Financial services
  • Oil & Gas
  • Telecoms
  • FMCG
  • Agriculture

Alignment with:

  • NESREA Act, SON Act, SEC Nigeria Guidelines
  • International frameworks (GRI, TCFD, ISSB, etc.)

4. Independent ESG Assurance & Audit

We provide objective third-party assurance to verify your ESG data and claims, building credibility and preventing greenwashing.

  • ESG and sustainability assurance (non-financial audit)
  • GHG inventory audit (Scopes 1–3)
  • Assurance of CSR claims
  • Greenwashing detection & integrity checks
  • Internal ESG maturity assessment
  • Readiness assessment for sustainability-linked loans

5. Carbon Footprint & Climate Risk Services

We measure your organization's carbon emissions and develop strategies to mitigate climate-related risks, guiding your transition to a low-carbon economy.

  • GHG accounting and carbon footprint calculation
  • Net-zero roadmap design
  • TCFD-aligned climate risk scenario analysis
  • Climate adaptation strategy
  • Emissions data modeling and visualization

6. Training & Capacity Building

We empower your team with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively implement and manage your ESG initiatives.

  • Executive workshops on ESG leadership
  • Staff training on ESG data management
  • Sector-specific ESG reporting training
  • Internal ESG/sustainability champions program
  • Boardroom briefings on ESG regulatory readiness
